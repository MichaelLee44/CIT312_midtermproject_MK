About Meals By Michael

Meals by Michael was founded by me, Michael Kellems. The idea behind Meals by Michael is that I am not a classically trained chef but I share a passion for home cooking. Through Meals by Michael, I try to share recipes I personally like to help foster the love of home cooking in other people. With that being said, lets get to cooking!

You can find recipes on here, curated by myself, and request my services for whatever catering or other chef based needs you need! For more inqueries, contact me at ml.kellems61@gmail.com or +1 317 427 3653.
